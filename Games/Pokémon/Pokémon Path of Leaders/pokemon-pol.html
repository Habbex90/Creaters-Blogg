Pokémon Path of Leaders

This is "Pokémon Path of Leaders" in this game you can choose between the short version and the long version. If you choose the short version you can choose whether you want to be a trainer who later becomes a champion or if you want to be a gym leader who later becomes an elite four member. If you choose the long version you will start as a trainer who later becomes a champion after that you will become a gym leader who later becomes an elite four member. (Under construction)
