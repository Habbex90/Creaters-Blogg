Pokémon: Path of Leaders


This is a unique fan-made Pokémon game (in my opinion). You can choose and play a short variation of the game
where you get to choose between being a Pokémon trainer who later becomes a champion or a gym leader who later becomes one of the elite four.
If you choose to play the long variation, you start as a trainer who becomes a champion.
After becoming a champion, you become a gym leader who later becomes one of the elite four.

Instruktioner

  • Function: Keys: Description:
  • Movement Arrow keys Move up, down, left, right
  • Confirm / Talk / Use menu selection Z / Enter / Space Select, talk to NPC, interact with objects
  • Cancel / Back / Close menu X / Esc Go back, close menu
  • Run Shift Hold to run (if you have Running Shoes)
  • Open menu X / Esc Opens main menu
  • Bike (if you have one) A Use bike
  • Hotkey for selected items D, F, S, A Can be configured to different shortcuts
  • Save game In the menu There is no hotkey, save via menu
